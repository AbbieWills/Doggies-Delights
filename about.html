What's in the tub

Indulge your furry friends with our exceptional ice cream, carefully crafted to prioritize their health and happiness. Our recipe revolves around fat-free yogurt, ensuring that each delightful scoop is as nutritious as can be, boasting a mere 76 calories per tub! We understand that dogs cannot digest lactose, which is why our formula is meticulously lactose-free, guaranteeing only happy tummies here!

Prepare to treat your beloved companions to a tantalizing selection of flavours. Currently, we offer banana flavour and peanut butter flavour (both made with real bananas and peanuts), with more enticing options on the horizon. Rest assured, our ice cream is exclusively concocted from 100% natural ingredients, completely free from added preservatives, sugar, or any nasties. We take pride in offering a guilt-free indulgence that radiates pure goodness. ❤

For optimal taste and texture, we recommend storing the ice cream in its frozen state at a chilly -18°C and consume within 5 months of purchase for optimal taste. Once defrosted, please consume within 3 days and do not refreeze.

Please note that while the ice cream baseline is fat-free, the peanut butter and bananas have naturally occurring fats.
